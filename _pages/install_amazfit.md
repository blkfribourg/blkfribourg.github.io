## Prerequisites
### A Compatible Amazfit device

- Active 2 (Round or Square, with or wthout NFC)
- Balance 2 (Round or Square)
- Bip 6
- T-Rex 3

### Zepp app installed on your phone

## Installation

1. Go to https://github.com/blkfribourg/WheelDash_Amazfit_Beta/tags and pick the version (latest is generally best)
2. Scroll to find your watch model
3. Open the zepp app and go to Profile > Settings > About, then repeatedly tap the Zepp app's icon (or version number) about 7-10 times until a confirmation pops up, which unlocks features like scanning QR codes for installing custom apps. 
4. In the Zepp app, go to Device > General > Developer Mode > Mini Program and click on + and select scan
5. Scan the Qcode found at step __2__
6. Wait a bit (leave the watch nearby) and you are done
